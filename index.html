What is a Computer?

A computer is an electronic device that processes data and performs tasks according to a set of instructions, called programs. It is capable of performing a wide range of tasks, such as calculations, data processing, communication, entertainment, and more. Computers can be found in various forms, such as desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. They have revolutionized nearly every aspect of modern life, from business and education to healthcare and entertainment.
